Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone
- Always verify links before clicking
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Always check website URLs before login
- Use official apps for UPI and payments
- Do not respond to unknown emails or messages
- Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 in case of fraud
